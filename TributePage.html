  • 2004 - Century City, Season 1 Episode 4

  • 2011 - Horrible Bosses
  • 2012 - Castle, Season 4 Episode 17. Dysfunctional Friends
  • 2013 - Random Encounters
  • 2014 - When Sparks Fly, Hallmark Channel TV Film
  • 2015 - Anti-Social
  • 2016 - Dater's Handbook, Hallmark Channel TV Film
  • 2011-2018 - Suits, Seasons 1-7
